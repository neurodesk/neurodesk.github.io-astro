Neurodesk relies on your support to cover our infrastructure costs. Support Neurodesk by choosing the donation option that matches your location:

## For users outside the United States

Donate securely through UQ Giving: **[Donate via UQ Giving](https://donations.uq.edu.au/EAINNEUR)** (opens in a new tab).

Thank you for supporting our project. You can run this command inside Neurodesk to remove the donation reminder banner:

```bash
touch ~/.config/neurodesk_supporter
```
