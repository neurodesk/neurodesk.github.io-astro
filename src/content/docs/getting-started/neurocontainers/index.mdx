Neurocontainers are individual containerised neuroimaging tools that you can pull and run directly with Docker, Singularity/Apptainer, or distribute over CVMFS. They are the building blocks behind Neurodesktop and the [Neurodesk App](/getting-started/local/neurodeskapp/), and you can also use them on their own in your own pipelines.

Containers are built automatically in the [neurocontainers repository](https://github.com/neurodesk/neurocontainers/) and published to [Docker Hub](https://hub.docker.com/u/vnmd), [GitHub Container Registry](https://github.com/neurodesk/neurocontainers/packages), and as Singularity images on object storage and CVMFS.
