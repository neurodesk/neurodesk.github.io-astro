:::note[Note]
Link to Nectar Virtual Desktop Service: **[https://desktop.rc.nectar.org.au](https://desktop.rc.nectar.org.au)**
:::

:::caution
This option is only available to Australian researchers. See [here](https://desktop.rc.nectar.org.au/about/) for more information.
:::

There are a few differences between the open-source version of Neurodesk and what's hosted on Nectar VDI:

1. There is no `/neurodesktop-storage` folder (the folder on the Desktop does not lead anywhere).
2. Files uploaded via drag and drop do not get stored on the desktop but in `/home/vdiuser/thinclient_drives/GUACFS`.

## Instructions for use

<Steps>

1. Go to [https://desktop.rc.nectar.org.au/](https://desktop.rc.nectar.org.au/).
2. Click on "Sign in".
3. Choose the AAF option.
4. Choose your institution from the list.
5. Provide your email address and password.
6. Click on create a Workspace (you only need to do this when you sign in for the first time).
   ![Create workspace](/docs/getting-started/neurodesktop/create_worksapce.png)
7. Fill in the form 'Apply for new Workspace' and submit.
   ![Apply for workspace](/docs/getting-started/neurodesktop/apply_for_workspace.png)
8. Click on "EXPLORE".
   ![Explore](/docs/getting-started/neurodesktop/Explore.png)
9. Click "VIEW DETAILS" under Neurodesktop.
10. Click "CREATE DESKTOP +" button on the top right corner.
11. Choose the desired availability zone.
12. Wait until everything is completed:
    ![Desktop ready](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/4021595/150286126-2d8f60af-7499-4c69-998a-668c9c34d8c9.png)
13. Click "OPEN DESKTOP ->":
    ![Open desktop](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/4021595/150286383-1c1b8d20-c772-4598-a81b-1ef9e11526b8.png)
14. For a general guide on using the ARDC virtual desktops, see [https://tutorials.rc.nectar.org.au/virtual-desktop-service/01-overview](https://tutorials.rc.nectar.org.au/virtual-desktop-service/01-overview).
15. For a specific explanation on how to launch the various applications available in the Neurodesktop desktop, follow the instructions at [/getting-started/neurodesktop/](/getting-started/neurodesktop/).

</Steps>
